## The Research Framework

Each company was evaluated in two stages.

### Stage 1 — Eligibility Gates

| Gate | What it checks |
|------|---------------|
| **E1: Producer** | Is the company an in-house manufacturer? (Not a trader, distributor, or CRO) |
| **E2: Accessible** | Does the company have confirmed operational presence in Pune? |

Both gates must pass before a company enters scoring.

### Stage 2 — Federer Score (C3–C8)

| Criterion | Max | What it measures |
|-----------|-----|-----------------|
| C3 Differentiation | 20 | Proprietary products, niche technical moat, certifications |
| C4 Decision-Maker | 15 | Founder/leadership quality and technical pedigree |
| C5 Growing Sector | 15 | Sector tailwinds — policy, China+1, export demand |
| C6 Growth Signals | 15 | Hiring, expansion, funding, certifications, recent activity |
| C7 Systems Maturity | 20 | ERP, ISO certifications, structured operations |
| C8 Leadership Succession | 15 | Gen-2 involvement, professional management depth |
| **Total** | **100** | |

### Bands

| Band | Score | Meaning |
|------|-------|---------|
| A | 75–100 | Strong Federer fit |
| B | 55–74 | Fit with minor gaps |
| C | 35–54 | Borderline / early stage |
| D | < 35 | Weak fit or insufficient evidence |

---

## Key Scoring Note

> Revenue **below** any threshold is **not a disqualifier**.  
> The only revenue-based disqualifier is exceeding **Rs.500Cr** annual revenue.  
> Low-revenue companies are scored on their technical merits and evidence, not penalized for size.

## How to Read the Spreadsheet

The spreadsheet has two sheets:

- **Federer Scores** — one row per company, all 32 fields
- **Legend & Scoring Guide** — scoring weights, band definitions, flag key

Each company row includes:
- Company name, website, segment, what they make, revenue band
- E1 and E2 verdict with one-line evidence
- C3–C8 label (Strong / Moderate / Weak), numeric score, and evidence note
- Total score, band, overall verdict
- Personalization hook — one specific, verifiable detail for use in outreach
- Flags — only real concerns (ownership, E1/E2 ambiguity, insufficient evidence)
